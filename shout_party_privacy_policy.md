# Privacy Policy — Shout Party

**Last updated: 25 May 2026**

## Overview

Shout Party ("we", "us", "our") is a word-guessing party game for Android, distributed on Google Play. This policy explains what data we collect, why, how we handle it, and the choices you have.

## Summary

- The game itself works entirely offline. Words, scores, and settings stay on your device.
- We use Google's **Firebase** services to understand how the app is used, fix crashes, and monitor performance. **All Firebase data collection is OFF by default** and only activates if you explicitly opt in on first launch or in Settings.
- We never collect your name, email, contacts, location, photos, or microphone audio.

## Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following are stored on your device only and never transmitted:

- Sound and haptic settings (on/off)
- Language preference
- Game history within the current session
- Your privacy choices (which Firebase services you've consented to)

Uninstalling the app removes all of this.

## Data Collected via Firebase (only with your consent)

When you first open Shout Party, you are shown a Privacy & Data screen with three toggles. All toggles are **OFF by default**. You may change them at any time from the in-app **Settings → Privacy & Data** screen.

### Firebase Analytics (off by default)

Purpose: understand which game modes are popular and how long sessions last, so we can improve the game.

Data collected when enabled:
- Anonymous Firebase installation ID (a randomly generated identifier specific to this app on this device)
- Device model, operating system version, app version, language, country (derived from IP at collection time)
- In-app events: screen views, game mode chosen, round completed, game finished, share button tapped, consent choices saved, settings changed, word reported (when you tap "🚩 Report a word" — we receive only the word's ID, the dictionary language, the reason you selected, and the game mode; your free-text comment is sent only via your own email client and not through analytics), interest in upcoming word packs (when you tap the "Word Packs" preview tile in Setup — we record only the tap), and deck exhaustion (when you finish all words in the current rotation during a session — we record the game mode, the number of words seen, and how many difficulty or category filters were active; no word IDs)
- We do **not** collect Android Advertising ID
- We do **not** collect names, emails, or any identifier that could identify you personally

### Firebase Crashlytics (off by default)

Purpose: receive automatic reports when the app crashes so we can fix bugs.

Data collected when enabled:
- Stack trace of the crash (which file and line of our code failed)
- Device model, OS version, app version, available memory at time of crash
- A randomly generated installation ID

No personal data, contacts, or in-game content is included in crash reports.

### Firebase Performance Monitoring (off by default)

Purpose: measure how fast the app starts and renders screens.

Data collected when enabled:
- App startup time, screen render time, frozen-frame rate
- Device model, OS version, app version
- A randomly generated installation ID

No personal data is included.

## How Firebase Data is Processed

Firebase services are operated by **Google LLC**. When you opt in, the data described above is transmitted to and processed by Google. Google's processing of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

We use Google's **Consent Mode v2** mechanism to ensure that no Firebase data is collected before you give consent.

## Your Choices and Rights

- **Withdraw consent at any time:** open Settings → Privacy & Data and disable the toggles. Disabling stops further collection immediately.
- **Access, deletion, or correction requests:** email privacy@sepulka.cc and we will action your request within 30 days. Because we do not collect names or emails, please include your Firebase installation ID (visible in Settings → Privacy & Data → "Your installation ID") so we can locate your records.
- **EU/EEA residents (GDPR):** you have the rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection, and the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
- **California residents (CCPA):** you have the right to know, delete, and opt out of "sale" of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

## Children

Shout Party is rated for ages 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app and you wish to delete any associated data, contact privacy@sepulka.cc.

## Game Content

The game dictionary contains approximately 1,500 words per language across 29 languages. Words are reviewed before release. If you encounter a word you find inappropriate, please contact privacy@sepulka.cc with the word and language; we will review and may remove it from future updates.

We are not liable for any distress caused by words in the game dictionary.

## In-App Purchases (planned, not in v1.0)

Future versions may offer optional word pack purchases via Google Play Billing. All payment processing is handled by Google. We do not receive or store your payment details. We will update this policy before enabling purchases.

## Data Retention

- **Locally on your device:** retained until you uninstall the app or clear app data.
- **Firebase Analytics data (if you opt in):** retained by Google for up to 14 months, after which it is automatically deleted by Google. See https://support.google.com/firebase/answer/7667196.
- **Crashlytics data (if you opt in):** retained for 90 days from the date of the crash.
- **Performance Monitoring data (if you opt in):** retained for 90 days.
